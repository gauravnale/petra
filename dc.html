Call for Doctoral Consortium Applications

PhD students who have at least one PETRA 2022 paper (Full paper, Short paper, Poster paper, or Workshop paper) and will graduate in or after Summer 2022 are eligible to apply for the doctoral consortium (DC).

The DC application submission deadline is March 29, 2022 and notification is on April 22, 2022. We encourage Ph.D. students who study at U.S. institutions to submit their applications as soon as possible. In the application form, you are required to upload:

  • Paper Abstract (up to 250 words)
  • Official Letter in English indicating your student status
  • Personal Statement in English ( 1 page max)
